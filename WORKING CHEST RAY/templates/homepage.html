Breaking Barriers in Pneumonia Diagnosis.

Our portable X-ray machine enables a chest X-ray to be taken in the field.

We are a not-for-profit organisation in the UK, dedicated to make health facilities accessible for all

About Us

We aim to improve global health by developing a practical tool for early and accurate pneumonia diagnosis in low-income regions. Our solution reduces dependence on traditional healthcare infrastructure and specialists, making detection more accessible and cost-effective. By leveraging technology, we empower providers in resource-limited settings, helping to lower mortality rates and improve patient outcomes.

Services

Visit your nearest chest-ray field clinic. Our health-workers will take an X-ray of your lungs. They will upload those X-rays onto our system. The system will then generate a response that would allow us to detect whether there re symptoms of pneumonia or not. In case of a high risk of pneumonia, our online Health Experts will check your reports and provide a feedback.
